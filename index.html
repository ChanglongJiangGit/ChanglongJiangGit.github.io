Changlong Jiang 姜昌龙

I am a master’s student at the Department of Artificial Intelligence and Automation, Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), advised by Prof. Yang Xiao. Before that, I received my bachelor's degree from HUST in 2022. I was born in Tai'an, Shandong, a beautiful historical and cultural city at the foot of Mount Tai.

My research interests include computer vision and pattern recognition. My current research direction is mainly in human body analysis, including human pose estimation and hand pose estimation.
